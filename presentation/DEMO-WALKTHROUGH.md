- Show the **+ New Move-Out** button and the form fields
- "The system tracks: requested date, reason, forwarding contact, and status progression"

**Workflow to explain:**
1. Resident requests move-out
2. Staff generates clearance (room inspection)
3. Final billing is computed (prorated charges)
4. Refund processed if applicable
5. Marked complete — room freed up for new tenant

## DEMO TIPS

1. **Keep the browser open** throughout the presentation — switch between slides and live app
2. **Use the sidebar navigation** to move between modules smoothly
3. **Don't create/modify data** during demo — use "Cancel" on forms to keep data consistent
4. **If asked about pricing:** Redirect to business discussion after demo
5. **If asked about customization:** "The platform is modular — we can configure it to match your specific building layout and billing structure"
6. **Mobile responsiveness:** If client asks, resize the browser window to show the responsive sidebar collapse
